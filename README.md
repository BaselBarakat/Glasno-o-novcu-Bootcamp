# Glasno-o-novcu-Bootcamp

Glasno o novcu ("Openly about money") is a financial literacy campaign built around the idea that personal finance is a skill anyone can learn — and one too few people are ever taught. The bootcamp is its creative engine: a structured 5-day programme in which participants move from strangers to a coordinated campaign team, building six real project components from scratch.

The bootcamp opens with team formation grounded in personality profiling, ensuring groups are balanced by working style rather than assembled by chance. From there, teams spend two days researching the financial literacy landscape, defining their audience, generating ideas, and stress-testing assumptions — before committing to a direction. The middle days shift into design and build: teams prototype, sprint, integrate their work with other components, and run live user tests. The week closes with polished 10-minute presentations to a panel, followed by a structured roadmap that gives each component a concrete path toward the live campaign.

The six components — a web platform, an interactive prize quiz, a digital advertising campaign, influencer collaborations, a digital platform development track, and 3D visualisation — are designed to function independently but reinforce each other. A quiz that feeds traffic to the platform. Ads that drive people to the quiz. Influencer content that speaks the same language as the editorial articles. Every team knows what the others are building and why it matters.

The bootcamp is designed for people who are new to campaign work, not veterans. The structured scaffolding — handouts, pitch templates, sprint checklists, user testing scripts — means participants spend their time thinking and building rather than figuring out what to do next. The facilitator role is consultative, not directive: teams own their decisions from Day 1.

By the end of the week, Glasno o novcu has moved from concept to a set of tested, demo-ready outputs — and the people who built them understand the campaign well enough to carry it forward.
